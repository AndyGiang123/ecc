Workshops

PERSONAL

This series encompasses the obstacles we all may face in everyday life and the affects of those things on our mental health. To combat these issues, our team has gathered a diverse group of women to discuss and advocate for acceptance and appreciation for your self-worth.

Taking a Non-Traditional Path as a Clinical Psychologist & Podcaster: Helping the Asian Community through Mental Health Care & Advocacy, and Story-Telling
Stephanie J. Wong
In this personal workshop, Dr. Stephanie J. Wong will share her journey that led to her current occupation as a clinical psychologist and podcaster. She will share inspiring guests' stories from The Color of Success Podcast. Dr. Wong will facilitate dialogue about mental health in the Taiwanese/Tawainese American community, which continues to be a stigmatized topic. Attendees can learn how to cope and maintain mental resilience when faced with daily obstacles, starting college, or transitioning to “adult life” after graduation. Attendees will explore how they can implement these coping skills following the workshop.
Overcoming Mental Health Stigma: Asian American Perspective
Michelle Yang
As an Asian American immigrant navigating life with bipolar disorder, Michelle Yang, MBA, realized she can’t advocate for herself, or anyone, if she doesn’t first admit her own struggles. Her determination to fight stigma inspired her to leave a successful career in corporate America and become a writer and advocate focused on the intersection of Asian American identity, feminism, and mental health. Her mission is to humanize and normalize the way we talk about mental health and demonstrate that thriving while living with a mental health condition is more than possible. Join us as we discuss ways to cope and share resources.
INTERPERSONAL

With the increase in Anti-Asian hate crimes as a result of COVID, these speakers touch upon the effects of the Model Minority Myth, highlight how to combat the labels that are embedded within Asian identities, and shed light how they can move forward together. They teach the attendees how to acknowledge our privileges as Asian Americans and how to help spread equity, equality, and inclusion.

CAREER

In this workshop series, attendees will be able to meet speakers on various career paths. Through the inspiring stories of our speakers, attendees will learn about how these speakers strayed away from others' expectations and paved their own pathway. Attendees may find reassurance about their future uncertainty or unexpected career paths.

Women & Minorities in Gaming: You Deserve to Sit at the Table
Morgan Ling
Have you ever thought about turning a passion into a career? Do you find the thought of someday switching career paths daunting? Or, at the very least, are you interested in learning what it’s like working in the games industry? Join Morgan Ling as she shares her journey, from studying something not super relevant (to gaming) to becoming a game developer, leading monetization for several new and upcoming games, at Riot Games (developer & publisher of League of Legends and Valorant). Her stories touch on both the highs and lows of being an Asian woman in a white male-dominated industry. She’ll talk about what it’s like being the only woman in the room, the effects of imposter syndrome, and how to challenge stereotypical definitions of success.
First Year to Founder: Career Myths, Misconceptions, and Matters of Fact
Grace Chi
Join Grace in a candid, insider discussion about building a modern career. Follow Grace — and hone your own skills — as she recounts lessons learned and barriers broken during her unconventional path from liberal arts student (and TASA member!) to cybersecurity cofounder.

This workshop will:
  • Demystify that first job and debunk myths about careers
  • Establish core foundations in the transition to being an employed “adult”
  • Provide clarity and practical methods to (authentically) boost career prospects
  • Share real-world examples on how to address job anxiety, build resilience, tackle stereotypes, and pivot towards success
