Workshops

personal, interpersonal, career
Personal.

This series encompasses the obstacles we all may face in everyday life and the affects of those things on our mental health. To combat these issues, our team has gathered a diverse group of women to discuss and advocate for acceptance and appreciation for your self-worth.

Interpersonal.

With the increase in Anti-Asian hate crimes as a result of COVID, these speakers touch upon the effects of the Model Minority Myth, highlight how to combat the labels that are embedded within Asian identities, and we they can move forward together. They teach the attendees how to acknowledge our privileges as Asian Americans and how to help spread equity, equality, and inclusion.

Career.

In this workshop series, attendees will be able to meet speakers on various career paths. Through the inspiring stories of our workshop speakers, attendees will learn about how these speakers strayed away from others' expectations and made their own pathway. Attendees may find reassurance about future uncertainty or unexpected career paths.
