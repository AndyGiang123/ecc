Location

City & Campus

RUTGERS UNIVERSITY, NEW BRUNSWICK

Rutgers–New Brunswick is one of three regional campuses of Rutgers University and is situated in central New Jersey, near the major urban centers of New York City and Philadelphia and the iconic Jersey Shore. Rutgers New Brunswick is home to five smaller campuses: College Avenue, Busch, Livingston, Cook, and Douglass. Each of the five campuses hosts its own specific academic departments, student center, libraries, dining halls, and residence buildings.

Main Venue

COLLEGE AVE STUDENT CENTER

126 College Ave, New Brunswick, NJ 08901

The main venue of ITASA ECC. Preferable given its proximity to the train station, Academic Building, food places, and other venues. Includes spacious lounges and a few food options such as Wendy’s and Subway.

Hotel

THE HELDRICH HOTEL

10 Livingston Ave, New Brunswick, NJ 08901

A 15 minute walk from campus, both hotels located in downtown New Brunswick provide amenities including free wifi and fitness centers. Both are located within a minute’s walk from popular restaurants and after-party venues.

Bus System

The Rutgers University-New Brunswick Campus Buses use ADA accessible buses on multiple routes across campus, providing free safe and reliable service to the Rutgers University community. Download the Rutgers University App for real time bus schedules.

Bus Route Names:

  • A/H: College Avenue and Busch
  • LX: College Avenue and Livingston
  • EE: College Avenue and Downtown New Brunswick
  • REXL: Cook/Douglas and Livingston
  • REXB: Cook/Douglass and Busch
  • F: Cook/Douglas and College Avenue
  • B/B-He: Livingston and Busch

Travel

PARKING

Visitors may park in the Yellow or Green Lot on the Livingston Campus during the weekends between Friday 5:00pm till Monday morning at 6am without a permission.

To park in other designated parking lots, a parking permit must be purchased from the Department of Transportation Services between the hours of 8:30am and 5:00pm Monday through Friday for the cost of $5.00 a day.

For visitors staying at the Heldrich Hotel, they may park in Morris Street Deck nearby.

AIRPLANE

If flying in, we advise guests to arrive at Newark Liberty International Airport and either taking the train or getting a ride to New Brunswick from there.

One-way fare from Newark Airport Train Station to New Brunswick Station: $17.50

TRAIN

Visitors can get off at New Brunswick Station, a railroad station near the College Avenue Campus.
